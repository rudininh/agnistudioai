# User Journey - Agni Studio AI

## Overview
The user journey outlines the end-to-end experience of a creator using Agni Studio AI, from initial onboarding to ongoing content creation and optimization. This journey helps identify key touchpoints, pain points, and opportunities for intervention.

## Stages

### 1. Onboarding
**Goal:** Set up workspace and define content strategy.
- **User Actions:**
  - Sign up and create account
  - Define niche, target audience, and content goals
  - Connect social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.)
  - Set content cadence and publishing schedule
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Welcome screen and tutorial
  - Niche selection wizard
  - Goal setting module
  - Calendar integration setup
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Excitement about starting fresh
  - Overwhelm from too many options
  - Uncertainty about defining niche and goals
- **Pain Points:**
  - Difficulty articulating niche and target audience
  - Confusion about optimal posting frequency
  - Technical difficulties connecting accounts
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Guided niche and audience definition based on trends and user interests
  - Template goals based on similar successful creators
  - One-click platform integrations with guided setup
  - AI-suggested cadence based on niche and goals

### 2. Discover
**Goal:** Generate and curate content ideas aligned with audience and trends.
- **User Actions:**
  - Browse AI-generated content ideas
  - Save ideas to idea bank
  - Research trending topics and keywords
  - Analyze competitor content (optional)
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Idea feed/dashboard
  - Keyword and trend explorer
  - Idea saving and tagging system
  - Competitor analysis tool (basic)
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Excitement when seeing relevant ideas
  - Frustration when ideas feel generic or off-brand
  - Curiosity about trending topics
  - Overwhelm from too many ideas
- **Pain Points:**
  - Ideas not aligned with personal style or audience
  - Difficulty validating idea potential before creation
  - Time-consuming manual research
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Personalized idea generation based on past performance and audience preferences
  - Idea scoring based on predicted performance and trend alignment
  - One-click saving and categorization of ideas
  - Integrated trend data from multiple platforms
  - Competitor gap analysis suggestions

### 3. Produce
**Goal:** Transform selected idea into production-ready plan.
- **User Actions:**
  - Select an idea from idea bank
  - Generate script, outline, and shot list
  - Customize AI-generated content to personal style
  - Add notes and references for production
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Idea detail view
  - Script and outline generator
  - Shot list and B-roll planner
  - Customization editor
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Satisfaction when AI captures their voice
  - Frustration when output needs heavy editing
  - Confidence from having a clear production plan
  - Anxiety about missing important elements
- **Pain Points:**
  - AI-generated content feels generic
  - Difficulty translating idea to actionable production plan
  - Forgetting important production elements (B-roll, hooks, CTAs)
  - Time spent editing AI output
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Style training: AI learns from user's past content to match tone and style
  - Modular generation: user can regenerate specific sections (hook, body, CTA)
  - Interactive storyboarding with drag-and-drop shot list
  - Smart reminders for commonly forgotten elements based on content type
  - Collaboration notes for team members

### 4. Plan
**Goal:** Schedule production and publishing tasks efficiently.
- **User Actions:**
  - Assign production tasks to calendar
  - Set reminders for shooting and editing sessions
  - Identify potential bottlenecks in production pipeline
  - Adjust timeline based on availability
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Content calendar view
  - Task assignment and reminder system
  - Bottleneck predictor
  - Schedule adjustment interface
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Relief from having a clear production timeline
  - Anxiety about potential delays
  - Satisfaction from seeing progress visualized
  - Frustration when unexpected events disrupt schedule
- **Pain Points:**
  - Underestimating time required for production tasks
  - Difficulty balancing content creation with other commitments
  - Lack of visibility into upcoming bottlenecks
  - Inflexible scheduling tools
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Time estimation based on content type and historical data
  - Automatic blocking of production time in calendar
  - Bottleneck prediction based on task dependencies and durations
  - Dynamic rescheduling with conflict detection
  - Integration with personal and team calendars

### 5. Produce (Execution)
**Goal:** Execute production plan efficiently.
- **User Actions:**
  - Follow production checklist during shooting
  - Capture all planned assets (A-roll, B-roll, audio, etc.)
  - Note deviations and issues during production
  - Backup and organize raw footage
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Mobile/desktop production checklist
  - Real-time asset tracking
  - Deviation logging
  - Backup and upload reminders
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Confidence from following a structured checklist
  - Frustration when interruptions occur
  - Relief when all assets are captured
  - Anxiety about missing shots despite checklist
- **Pain Points:**
  - Forgetting to check off items on checklist
  - Difficulty adapting checklist to changing conditions on set
  - No easy way to mark missing assets for later capture
  - Tedious manual logging of production notes
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Context-aware checklist that adapts to shooting environment
  - Voice-activated checklist progress tracking
  - AI-assisted shot suggestion based on scene and available equipment
  - Automatic flagging of potential missing assets
  - Voice-to-text production notes

### 6. Post-produce
**Goal:** Edit and prepare content for publishing.
- **User Actions:**
  - Import and organize footage
  - Follow editing guide for pacing, transitions, and effects
  - Generate captions, hashtags, and title suggestions
  - Select or generate thumbnail options
  - Review final cut with AI feedback
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Editing timeline with suggested cuts
  - Caption and hashtag generator
  - Title optimizer
  - Thumbnail generator and A/B tester
  - Pre-publish review checklist
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Overwhelm from editing decisions
  - Satisfaction from AI-suggested improvements
  - Uncertainty about thumbnail effectiveness
  - Relief when workflow is guided
- **Pain Points:**
  - Spending too much time on minor editing decisions
  - Lack of data-driven guidance for captions and hashtags
  - Guesswork in thumbnail selection
  - Inconsistent application of branding elements
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Edit decision suggestions based on retention data
  - Caption and hashtag generation optimized for platform algorithms
  - Title scoring based on click-through rate prediction
  - Thumbnail generation with facial expression and text readability analysis
  - Brand consistency checker for colors, fonts, and logos

### 7. Publish
**Goal:** Publish content at optimal time with effective metadata.
- **User Actions:**
  - Review final metadata (title, description, tags)
  - Schedule publish time or publish immediately
  - Set up cross-posting to other platforms (if desired)
  - Enable notifications for performance tracking
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Metadata review and optimization interface
  - Publishing scheduler
  - Cross-platform sharing settings
  - Notification preferences
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Confidence from optimized metadata
  - Anxiety about publishing at the wrong time
  - Satisfaction from seamless cross-platform sharing
  - Relief from automated publishing
- **Pain Points:**
  - Uncertainty about optimal publishing time
  - Tedious manual metadata entry for multiple platforms
  - Inconsistent branding across platforms
  - Missing opportunistic timing for trending topics
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Optimal publishing time prediction based on audience activity
  - Automated metadata generation and platform-specific optimization
  - Brand template application across platforms
  - Real-time trend jacking suggestions
  - One-click scheduling to multiple platforms

### 8. Learn
**Goal:** Analyze performance and derive actionable insights.
- **User Actions:**
  - Review performance dashboard after publishing
  - Identify key insights from analytics
  - Determine next steps based on performance
  - Save learnings for future content planning
- **Touchpoints:**
  - Performance dashboard with key metrics
  - Insight generator (AI-powered analysis)
  - Action recommendation engine
  - Learning journal / knowledge base
- **Thoughts & Emotions:**
  - Confusion from raw analytics data
  - Excitement when discovering clear insights
  - Frustration from unclear next steps
  - Satisfaction from actionable recommendations
- **Pain Points:**
  - Difficulty connecting metrics to specific content elements
  - Lack of guidance on what metrics matter most for goals
  - Overwhelm from negative or stagnant performance
  - Inability to recall past learnings when planning new content
- **Opportunities for Agni Studio AI:**
  - Automated insight generation highlighting significant changes
  - Root cause analysis for performance changes
  - Personalized action recommendations (e.g., "Try shorter intros")
  - Pattern detection across multiple videos
  - Searchable knowledge base of past learnings

## Key Touchpoints Across Journey
- **Dashboard/Home:** Central hub showing upcoming tasks, idea feed, and performance snapshot
- **Calendar:** Integrated scheduling with production and publishing tasks
- **Creation Studio:** Where ideas are turned into production plans
- **Production Assistant:** Mobile/web guide during shooting
- **Editing Suite:** Guided post-production with AI suggestions
- **Analytics Hub:** Performance review and insight generation

## Pain Points & Opportunities Summary
| Stage | Primary Pain Points | Key Opportunities |
|-------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Onboarding | Niche definition, goal setting, platform setup | Guided setup, template goals, smart integrations |
| Idea Generation | Generic ideas, validation difficulty, research time | Personalized ideas, prediction scoring, trend integration |
| Production Planning | Generic output, missing elements, editing time | Style training, modular generation, smart reminders |
| Scheduling | Time estimation, bottlenecks, inflexibility | Predictive timing, bottleneck alerts, dynamic rescheduling |
| Production Execution | Forgotten checklist items, rigid checklists, manual notes | Context-aware checklists, voice control, AI suggestions |
| Post-production | Editing overwhelm, weak metadata, thumbnail guesswork | Edit suggestions, optimized metadata, thumbnail AI |
| Publishing | Suboptimal timing, manual metadata, inconsistent branding | Optimal timing, auto-metadata, brand templates |
| Performance Analysis | Confusing data, unclear next steps, overwhelmed by negatives | Automated insights, root cause analysis, action recommendations |

## Success Indicators
- Users report feeling guided rather than restricted by AI suggestions
- Reduction in time spent on ideation and planning by 40%
- Increase in on-time publishing from 50% to 80%
- Improvement in content performance metrics (views, engagement) by 30% after 3 months of use
- High retention of produced assets (less than 10% of planned assets missed)
