# Product Roadmap - Agni Studio AI

## Vision
To empower video creators with AI-powered tools that streamline the entire content lifecycle from ideation to analytics, enabling consistent, high-quality content production that grows audience and revenue.

## Strategic Themes
1. **Workflow Foundation** - Core content creation pipeline
2. **Intelligence Layer** - AI-powered insights and recommendations
3. **Automation & Scale** - Reducing manual effort through automation
4. **Collaboration & Expansion** - Team features and platform expansion

## Phase-by-Phase Plan

### Q1: Foundation & Workflow (Months 1-3)
**Goal:** Establish core content workflow for individual creators.

**Key Features:**
- Workspace setup (niche, goals, platform connections)
- Idea generation engine (trend-based, personalized)
- Script and outline generator
- Production checklist (mobile/web)
- Basic calendar scheduling
- Simple publishing to primary platform (YouTube)

**Success Metrics:**
- 70% of users complete onboarding
- 50% of users generate at least 3 ideas in first week
- 40% of users complete first content piece using platform
- Average time to produce first content reduced by 30%

### Q2: Intelligence & Analytics (Months 4-6)
**Goal:** Add AI-driven insights and analytics to improve content performance.

**Key Features:**
- Performance dashboard (views, engagement, retention)
- AI-powered insight generation (what worked, what didn't)
- Title and thumbnail optimizer
- Basic hashtag and caption suggestions
- Content performance prediction for ideas
- A/B testing framework for thumbnails/titles

**Success Metrics:**
- 60% of users check analytics after publishing
- 40% acceptance rate of AI-suggested titles/thumbnails
- 25% improvement in average video performance vs. baseline
- 50% of users implement at least one AI suggestion per video

### Q3: Automation & Collaboration (Months 7-9)
**Goal:** Reduce manual effort through automation and enable basic team collaboration.

**Key Features:**
- Automated publishing scheduler (optimal timing)
- Cross-platform publishing (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram)
- Team workspace (invite members, assign roles)
- Approval workflow for content
- Asset library and template management
- Automated reminder system

**Success Metrics:**
- 50% of users schedule content in advance
- 30% reduction in time spent on publishing tasks
- 40% of teams use collaboration features
- 60% of users report reduced stress from missed deadlines
- 35% increase in weekly content output per user

### Q4: Scale & Platform Expansion (Months 10-12)
**Goal:** Enable scaling for growing creators and expand to additional content formats and platforms.

**Key Features:**
- Brand kit management (logos, colors, fonts)
- Advanced analytics (cohort analysis, comparative benchmarking)
- Content repurposing suggestions (long-form to short-form)
- Additional platform integrations (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- API access for advanced users
- Template marketplace (community sharing)

**Success Metrics:**
- 50% of power users utilize brand kit features
- 30% of users repurpose content using suggestions
- 25% adoption rate for new platform integrations
- 20% of teams upgrade to collaborative features
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) > 40

## Long-Term Vision (Year 2+)
- Advanced AI co-creation (real-time scripting during filming)
- Predictive trend forecasting (emerging topics before they peak)
- Automated short-form content generation from long-form
- Brand partnership marketplace
- Advanced team workflows (role-based permissions, advanced analytics)
- Multi-language support and localization

## Release Principles
1. **User-Centered:** Every feature validated with creator interviews and testing
2. **Data-Driven:** Metrics-driven decision making for feature prioritization
3. **Iterative:** Release early, learn fast, improve continuously
4. **Sustainable:** Focus on features that provide ongoing value, not one-time novelty
5. **Integrated:** Features work seamlessly together in the content lifecycle

## Risk Mitigation
- **Technical Risk:** Invest in scalable cloud infrastructure early; use microservices architecture
- **Adoption Risk:** Start with core workflow, add complexity gradually; extensive onboarding
- **Competition:** Focus on AI integration and workflow specificity for video creators
- **Monetization:** Freemium model with clear value progression from free to paid tiers

## Success Metrics (North Star)
**Monthly Active Creators (MAC)** - Number of unique creators who publish at least one piece of content via the platform in a month.

**Secondary Metrics:**
- Activation Rate: % of new users who create their first content within 7 days
- Retention Rate (Day 30): % of users still active after 30 days
- Content Velocity: Average number of content pieces published per creator per week
- Performance Lift: Average improvement in key metrics (views, engagement) vs. pre-platform baseline
- Monetization Readiness: % of creators who reach platform-specific monetization thresholds

## Review Process
This roadmap will be reviewed and updated quarterly based on:
- User feedback and research
- Market trends and competitive landscape
- Technical feasibility and resource availability
- Business goals and metrics performance
