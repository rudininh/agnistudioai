# Success Metrics - Agni Studio AI

## North Star Metric
**Monthly Active Creators (MAC)** - Number of unique creators who publish at least one piece of content via the platform in a month.

*Why this metric?* It captures both adoption (users) and engagement (actual content creation/publishing), which is the core value proposition of helping creators produce consistently.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Acquisition & Activation
- **Activation Rate**: Percentage of new users who create and publish their first content within 7 days of signup
  - Target: >60% by end of Year 1
  - Measurement: Track cohort signups and first publish event within 7 days
- **Time to First Publish (TTP)**: Average time from account creation to first published content
  - Target: <3 days by end of Year 1
  - Measurement: Timestamp difference between signup and first publish event
- **Activation Cohort Retention**: Percentage of activated users (published in first 7 days) still active at 30 days
  - Target: >50% 
  - Measurement: Cohort analysis of activated users

### Engagement & Retention
- **Weekly Active Creators (WAC)**: Number of unique creators who engage with core features (idea generation, scheduling, analytics) in a week
  - Target: WAC/MAC ratio > 40%
  - Measurement: Weekly active user tracking
- **Content Frequency**: Average number of content pieces published per creator per week
  - Target: Increase from baseline 1.0 to 2.5+ by month 6
  - Measurement: Publish events per user per week
- **Feature Adoption Rate**: Percentage of active users using key features (AI ideas, scheduling, analytics) regularly
  - Target: >50% for core features by month 6
  - Measurement: Feature-specific usage tracking
- **30-Day Retention**: Percentage of users still active 30 days after signup
  - Target: >40% by end of Year 1
  - Measurement: Cohort retention analysis

### Monetization & Value
- **Creator Satisfaction (NPS)**: Net Promoter Score from user surveys
  - Target: >40 by end of Year 1
  - Measurement: Quarterly NPS surveys
- **Perceived Value Score**: Self-reported rating of how much the platform helps achieve content goals (1-10)
  - Target: >7.0 average
  - Measurement: Monthly surveys
- **Willingness to Pay (WTP)**: Percentage of free users who indicate they would pay for premium features
  - Target: >30% by end of Year 1
  - Measurement: Surveys and pricing tests

### Performance Impact
- **Content Performance Lift**: Average improvement in key metrics (views, watch time, engagement) for content created with platform vs. baseline
  - Target: 25%+ improvement by month 6
  - Measurement: Compare performance of platform-assisted content vs. pre-platform content for same creators
- **Algorithm Favorability Score**: Platform-specific score indicating algorithmic promotion (varies by platform)
  - YouTube: Increase in suggested traffic percentage
  - TikTok: Increase in "For You" appearance rate
  - Target: 15% improvement in key distribution metrics
  - Measurement: Platform analytics comparison
- **Retention Improvement**: Increase in average audience retention percentage
  - Target: 5-10 percentage point improvement
  - Measurement: Compare retention curves

### Operational Efficiency
- **Time Savings**: Reduction in total time spent per content piece from ideation to publish
  - Target: 40% reduction by month 6
  - Measurement: Time-tracking studies and user self-reports
- **Cognitive Load Reduction**: Self-reported reduction in mental effort and decision fatigue
  - Target: 35% reduction in reported overwhelm
  - Measurement: Regular surveys using NASA-TLX or similar
- **Consistency Improvement**: Reduction in standard deviation of publishing intervals
  - Target: 50% more consistent publishing schedule
  - Measurement: Standardization of inter-publish times

## Leading Indicators (Predictive Metrics)
These metrics predict future success and help us course-correct early:

- **Idea Generation Velocity**: Number of ideas generated per user per week
  - Predicts: Future content pipeline health
- **Schedule Adherence Rate**: Percentage of scheduled content that is published on time
  - Predicts: Long-term consistency and reliability
- **AI Suggestion Acceptance Rate**: Percentage of AI-generated suggestions (titles, tags, timing) that users accept
  - Predicts: Value perception and trust in AI
- **Feature Depth Index**: Average number of different features used per active user per week
  - Predicts: Engagement and likelihood of continued subscription
- **Content Completeness Rate**: Percentage of planned elements (shots, B-roll, etc.) actually captured
  - Predicts: Production quality and reduction in reshoots
- **Early Performance Signal**: Performance in first 1-2 hours after publishing (relative to historical average)
  - Predicts: Long-term performance trajectory

## Lagging Indicators (Outcome Metrics)
These measure the ultimate outcomes we're trying to achieve:

- **Subscriber/Follower Growth Rate**: Month-over-month growth in audience size
  - Target: 15%+ MoM growth for active users
- **Engagement Rate Growth**: Increase in likes, comments, shares per view
  - Target: 10%+ improvement
- **Watch Time/Average View Duration Increase**: 
  - Target: 15%+ increase
- **Revenue Milestone Achievement**: Percentage of creators reaching platform monetization thresholds
  - YouTube: 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours
  - TikTok: Creator Fund eligibility
  - Target: 25% of active users reach monetization within 6 months
- **Brand Deal Attribution**: Number of sponsored content pieces traced to platform use
  - Target: Baseline measurement then growth
- **Reduced Content Abandonment**: Percentage decrease in started-but-not-finished projects
  - Target: 50% reduction

## Health Metrics
These indicate the overall health of the product and business:

- **System Reliability**: Uptime and performance of core services
  - Target: 99.9% uptime
  - Measurement: Monitoring and alerting systems
- **Error Rate**: Frequency of user-encountered bugs or errors
  - Target: <1% of sessions have errors
  - Measurement: Error tracking and crash reporting
- **Support Ticket Volume**: Number of support requests per active user
  - Target: Decreasing over time as usability improves
  - Measurement: Support system analytics
- **Churn Rate**: Monthly percentage of users who stop being active
  - Target: <5% monthly churn
  - Measurement: Cohort-based churn analysis
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to acquire a paying user
  - Target: < LTV/3 (where LTV is lifetime value)
  - Measurement: Marketing spend divided by new paying users
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Predicted net profit from the entire future relationship with a user
  - Target: Increasing over time
  - Measurement: (ARPU * Gross Margin) / Churn Rate

## Measurement Framework

### Data Collection
1. **Product Telemetry**: Anonymous, aggregated usage tracking for feature adoption, performance, and workflow metrics
2. **User Surveys**: Regular NPS, CSAT, and specialized surveys for deeper insights
3. **Platform Analytics**: Integration with YouTube Studio, TikTok Analytics, etc., with user permission
4. **Cohort Analysis**: Group users by signup date to track changes over time
5. **A/B Testing**: Experimentally measure impact of new features and changes
6. **Qualitative Research**: Monthly user interviews and usability studies

### Reporting Cadence
- **Real-time Dashboards**: System health and critical alerts
- **Daily**: Key usage metrics and anomaly detection
- **Weekly**: Engagement trends and feature adoption
- **Monthly**: Executive KPI dashboard, cohort reports, and experiment results
- **Quarterly**: Strategic review against OKRs and business goals
- **Annually**: Comprehensive impact assessment and benchmarking

## Success Tracking by Timeline

### Month 1-3 (Launch & Learn)
- Focus: Activation, basic usability, core value validation
- Key Metrics: 
  - Activation Rate > 40%
  - TTP < 5 days
  - Core feature usage (idea gen, publishing) > 30% of Actives
  - NPS > 20
  - No critical system downtime

### Month 4-6 (Growth & Optimization)
- Focus: Engagement, performance impact, retention
- Key Metrics:
  - WAC/MAU > 35%
  - Content Frequency > 1.5x baseline
  - Performance Lift > 15%
  - 30-Day Retention > 30%
  - AI Suggestion Acceptance > 30%

### Month 7-9 (Scale & Monetization Prep)
- Focus: Expansion, team features, monetization readiness
- Key Metrics:
  - Team feature adoption > 20% of relevant users
  - Cross-publishing usage > 25%
  - WTP > 25%
  - Performance Lift > 20%
  - NPS > 35

### Month 10-12 (Maturity & Optimization)
- Focus: Optimization, efficiency, market position
- Key Metrics:
  - Content Frequency > 2.5x baseline
  - Time Savings > 35%
  - Performance Lift > 25%
  - NPS > 40
  - Monthly Churn < 6%
  - Ready for monetization launch

## Benchmarking & Baselines
We will establish baselines during onboarding:
- **Content Baseline**: Average publish frequency, time investment, performance metrics for last 3 months pre-platform
- **Process Baseline**: Self-reported time spent on ideation, research, planning, production, editing, publishing, analytics
- **Satisfaction Baseline**: Current satisfaction with tools and process (1-10 scale)
- **Pain Point Baseline**: Frequency and severity of specific pain points (idea block, scheduling conflicts, etc.)

## Statistical Significance & Confidence
- Minimum detectable effect (MDE) set at 10% for primary metrics
- Experiments run with 95% confidence level
- Minimum sample size calculated per experiment
- Sequential testing to avoid peeking

## Ethical Considerations
- All data collection is opt-in and anonymized where possible
- Users can delete their data and opt out of tracking
- No use of personal data for advertising without explicit consent
- Transparency about what data is collected and how it's used
- Regular audits for bias in AI recommendations

## Reporting & Transparency
- Monthly internal dashboard shared with entire team
- Quarterly public transparency report on key metrics (aggregated, anonymized)
- Annual impact report highlighting creator success stories and aggregated outcome data
- Real-time status page for system performance
