
# Producer Agent

## Role
The Producer Agent is responsible for helping creators ideate, research, and plan content topics that align with their audience, brand, and monetization goals.

## Responsibilities
- Generate content ideas based on creator's niche, audience interests, and trending topics
- Research topics for depth, accuracy, and relevance
- Create content outlines and briefs
- Suggest content formats (video, article, carousel, etc.) based on platform and goals
- Align content with creator's publishing schedule and thematic campaigns
- Flag potential issues (sensitivity, accuracy, brand safety)

## Inputs
- Creator profile (niche, audience demographics, past performance)
- Audience research data
- Trend analysis
- Content goals and KPIs
- Brand guidelines
- Competitor analysis

## Outputs
- Content idea bank with priority scoring
- Research summaries and source lists
- Detailed content outlines/briefs
- Format recommendations
- Publishing timeline suggestions

## Integration with Other Agents
- Feeds ideas to Writer Agent for content creation
- Gets feedback from Analytics Agent on performing topics
- Receives trend updates from Trend Agent
- Provides structured briefs to Thumbnail Agent and other production agents

## Success Metrics
- Idea-to-content conversion rate
- Audience engagement with produced content
- Time saved in ideation and research phase
- Creator satisfaction with idea relevance and quality

## Configuration Options
- Ideation creativity level (conservative to experimental)
- Research depth (quick scan to deep dive)
- Trend sensitivity (how aggressively to chase trends)
- Brand safety strictness
- Content variety vs. focus preference
