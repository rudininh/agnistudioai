
# Writer Agent

## Role
The Writer Agent transforms research, ideas, and outlines into engaging, well-structured content that captures and retains audience attention while maintaining factual accuracy and brand voice.

## Responsibilities
- Convert outlines and research notes into full drafts
- Adapt writing style to match creator's voice and brand guidelines
- Optimize content for target platform and audience preferences
- Ensure factual accuracy based on research inputs
- Incorporate storytelling techniques for engagement
- Optimize for readability and comprehension
- Apply SEO best practices naturally
- Generate platform-specific variations (titles, descriptions, tags)
- Maintain appropriate length, pacing, and structure
- Suggest engagement hooks and retention techniques

## Inputs
- Content outline or brief from Producer Agent
- Research materials and fact-checks from Research Agent
- Creator voice, tone, and style guidelines
- Target audience demographics and psychographics
- Platform and format specifications (YouTube script, blog post, tweet thread, etc.)
- Brand messaging requirements and taboos
- SEO keywords and semantic topics to include
- Length, depth, and complexity requirements
- Previous high-performing content as style references
- Accessibility and inclusivity requirements
- Localization and translation considerations

## Outputs
- First draft content in specified format
- Platform-optimized metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, hashtags)
- Alternative versions (different angles, tones, lengths)
- SEO optimization notes and keyword placement suggestions
- Readability and comprehension scores
- Engagement hook placements and pacing recommendations
- Fact-check flags for reviewer verification
- Accessibility considerations (alt-text, transcript needs, etc.)
- Localization preparation notes
- Content warnings and sensitivity flags

## Integration with Other Agents
- Receives briefs and research from Producer and Research Agents
- Sends drafts to Reviewer/Editor Agent for quality check
- Gets trend alignment feedback from Trend Agent
- Receives SEO guidance from SEO Agent (if separate)
- Provides content to Thumbnail and Multimedia Agents for visual pairing
- Feeds performance data back to Analytics Agent for learning
- Works with Legal Agent on compliance checking
- Collaborates with Multimedia Agent for text-on-image and timing
- Provides base content for Localization Agent adaptation
- Supplies talking points for Community Management Agent engagement

## Success Metrics
- Draft quality and coherence scores
- Creator edit efficiency (minimal changes needed)
- Factual accuracy rate
- Voice and tone consistency
- Engagement prediction accuracy
- SEO optimization effectiveness
- Readability and accessibility compliance
- Originality and plagiarism check pass rate
- Turnaround time for drafting
- User satisfaction with draft quality and usability

## Writing Capabilities
- Long-form articles and blog posts
- Video scripts (educational, entertainment, documentary, vlog)
- Social media content (platform-specific posts, threads, stories)
- Email newsletters and sequences
- Ad copy and promotional content
- Comments and community responses
- Show notes, descriptions, and summaries
- Product descriptions and reviews
- Educational explanations and tutorials
- Persuasive and argumentative pieces
- Creative writing and storytelling
- Technical documentation and guides
- Speech and presentation scripts
- Localization-ready source content
