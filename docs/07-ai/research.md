# Research Agent

## Role
The Research Agent gathers, synthesizes, and validates information to support content creation, ensuring accuracy, depth, and relevance while saving creators time on information gathering.

## Responsibilities
- Conduct comprehensive research on given topics
- Fact-check and verify information from multiple sources
- Identify credible and authoritative sources
- Summarize complex information into digestible formats
- Track trending topics and emerging developments
- Provide context and background information
- Identify gaps in existing content and opportunities for unique angles
- Maintain research logs and source tracking for credibility
- Adapt research depth based on content requirements and deadlines
- Ensure compliance with copyright and attribution requirements

## Inputs
- Research brief or topic specification
- Depth and breadth requirements (quick fact-check vs. deep dive)
- Time constraints and deadlines
- Target audience and knowledge level
- Content format and length requirements
- Preferred sources and source quality thresholds
- Previous research on related topics
- Specific questions or angles to explore
- Format requirements for output (bullet points, summary, etc.)
- Language and localization requirements
- Copyright and usage restriction guidelines

## Outputs
- Comprehensive research summaries
- Fact-checked and sourced information
- Source credibility assessments and rankings
- Key statistics and data points with citations
- Expert opinions and authoritative viewpoints
- Historical context and background information
- Current developments and trending aspects
- Counterarguments and alternative perspectives
- Related topics and further research suggestions
- Source lists with proper attribution formatting
- Confidence levels for different information points
- Visual elements suggestions (charts, graphs, images)

## Integration with Other Agents
- Provides research foundation to Writer Agent for content creation
- Supplies trend data and validation to Trend Agent
- Fact-checks content from Writer Agent before publishing
- Provides background information to Analytics Agent for context
- Supplies data for Educational and Explainer Content specialization
- Works with Legal/Compliance Agent on source usage rights
- Provides metadata for SEO Agent (keywords, topics, entities)
- Supplies background for Multimedia Agent (visual concepts, data viz)
- Informs Localization Agent about cultural context and adaptations
- Provides research depth guidance to Producer Agent for briefing

## Success Metrics
- Research accuracy and fact-check score
- Source credibility and diversity measures
- Information completeness for given depth level
- Time efficiency (research time vs. depth achieved)
- Citation and attribution completeness
- Bias detection and balance scoring
- Relevance to content objectives
- Plagiarism and originality verification
- User satisfaction with research depth and usability
- Turnaround time adherence to deadlines

## Research Methodologies
- Desk research and literature review
- Expert consultation and interview synthesis
- Data analysis and statistical interpretation
- Trend analysis and forecasting
- Comparative analysis and benchmarking
- Case study and example collection
- Historical research and archival investigation
- Scientific and technical paper review
- News and media monitoring
- Social listening and community insight gathering
- Patent and technical documentation review
- Academic and scholarly source evaluation
- Government and official source verification
- Multi-source triangulation for verification
